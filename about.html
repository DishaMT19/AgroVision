About AgroVision

AI-powered plant disease detection for farmers and gardeners

AgroVision is an AI-powered tool to help farmers and gardeners identify plant diseases quickly. Upload a leaf photo and get instant analysis. Our database covers hundreds of plant species and diseases.

🌱 Our Mission

We aim to democratize access to plant disease diagnosis, helping growers worldwide protect their crops and improve food security through accessible, AI-driven technology.

🔬 How It Works

AgroVision uses deep learning models trained on thousands of labeled leaf images. When you upload a photo, our AI analyzes visual patterns and compares them against our disease database to provide accurate identification and treatment recommendations.

📊 Our Impact

Since launching, AgroVision has helped identify diseases in over 10,000 plant samples across 30+ countries, enabling early intervention and reducing crop losses by an estimated 25%.
